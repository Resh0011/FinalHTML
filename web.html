Welcome!

LAZVM Coffee is a cozy, locally owned café serving ethically sourced coffee, handcrafted espresso drinks, and fresh, homemade pastries. Whether you're grabbing a quick morning boost or settling in for an afternoon of remote work, LAZVM Coffee offers a warm, welcoming space for all coffee lovers.

Shortly About Us

At LAZVM Coffee, we believe that coffee is more than just a drink — it's a daily ritual, a moment of pause, and a reason to connect. Founded in 2015, our mission has always been to bring people together over expertly crafted coffee in a cozy, welcoming environment.

We source our beans from sustainable farms around the world and roast them locally to ensure every cup is fresh and full of flavor. Whether you're here for a quiet morning brew, a casual meeting, or just a peaceful corner to read, LAZVM Coffee is your neighborhood retreat.

Come in for the coffee, stay for the comfort — and maybe a muffin or two.

Most Popular Coffees

Latte
A smooth and creamy espresso-based drink made with steamed milk and a thin layer of foam, perfect for those who enjoy a mild coffee flavor with a velvety texture.
Cappuccino
A classic Italian coffee drink made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and thick, frothy foam, offering a rich and bold flavor with a creamy texture.
Americano
A bold and smooth coffee made by adding hot water to a shot of espresso, resulting in a lighter, more diluted flavor similar to drip coffee but with espresso’s rich taste.
